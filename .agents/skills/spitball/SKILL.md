# Spitballing Skill

Take a look at some rough notes and help figure out the thrust of a blog post

## Usage

```
/spitball
```

## Instructions

Read through the draft I'm currently working on. Also read a few recent blog posts to get a feel for my style and content. Then help me figure out what the draft is about, or iterate on it to make it stronger. 

Do:
- Give a summary of the draft, focusing on the points that are strongest, most interesting, and/or best supported
- Note ways to re-order sections or paragraphs to improve flow
- Call out gaps in between the points that could derail the flow. Suggest possible topics to fill them
- Make note of content that does not seem to fit with the top-level summary

This is a rough draft! Do not worry about:
- Duplicate or near-duplicate content
- Issues with formatting, grammar, spelling, or syntax

## Output Format

Keep it terse

Wet ingredients:
- 1 can chickpeas, drained
- 4 overripe bananas
- 3/4 cup peanut butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

Dry ingredients:
- 2 cup oats
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1.5 tsp baking powder
- 1.5 tsp baking soda
- salt[^salt]
- 3 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

[^salt]: Canned beans are usually salted. Peanut butter is usually salted. Just a sprinkle is probably fine, if any. 

---

Preheat your oven to 350 F. 




Drain and rinse your chickpeas. Mash them or process them in a food processor. Add to a big bowl with the rest of your wet ingredients.
